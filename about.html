Mansi Shah

Mansi Shah, the visionary behind Soul Attitude Couture, is passionate about blending traditional Indian craftsmanship with contemporary Indo-Western fashion. Her designs reflect elegance, confidence, and individuality.

Soul Attitude Couture was founded to bring timeless Indian artistry to modern wardrobes. Each piece is handcrafted with attention to detail, making every outfit unique. Our brand celebrates the harmony of tradition and trend, giving every wearer a touch of sophistication and attitude.

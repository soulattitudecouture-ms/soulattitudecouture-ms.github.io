Our Story

Soul Attitude Couture is a contemporary fashion label rooted in individuality, craftsmanship, and effortless elegance. Each piece is thoughtfully designed to reflect confidence, modern femininity, and personal style.

The brand blends refined silhouettes with artisanal detailing, creating collections that feel both expressive and wearable. Produced in limited quantities, every design emphasizes quality, exclusivity, and attention to detail.

Designed in India, Soul Attitude Couture draws inspiration from heritage techniques while embracing a modern, global aesthetic.

Founded: India

Specialisation: Indo-western silhouettes & contemporary couture

Production: Limited-run, detail-driven craftsmanship

About the Founder

Founded by Mansi Shah, Soul Attitude Couture is driven by the belief that fashion is more than clothing — it is a form of self-expression. Her design philosophy focuses on creating pieces that empower individuals to dress with intention and confidence.

Every collection reflects a balance of creativity and precision, celebrating craftsmanship, authenticity, and timeless design.

Design: Contemporary, expressive, wearable

Craft: Detail-driven, small-batch production

Philosophy: Style with individuality and purpose

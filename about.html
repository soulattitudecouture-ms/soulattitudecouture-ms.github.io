Mansi Shah

Mansi Shah is the creative visionary behind Soul Attitude Couture. Combining contemporary Indo-Western aesthetics with sophisticated tailoring, she creates pieces that blend tradition and modern elegance. Each design reflects her passion for craftsmanship and attention to detail, making every garment a statement of style and personality.

With a focus on luxury fabrics and unique silhouettes, Mansi ensures that every collection is a reflection of both heritage and contemporary fashion trends. Her designs are perfect for those who wish to express individuality through timeless fashion.
